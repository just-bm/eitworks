Our Product

Our devices are precision-engineered for reliability and performance. We enable tracking, connecting, and automating
systems. Each device merges smart tech with rugged design for lasting impact.

AIS 140 is a set of standards published
by ARAI (Automotive Research
Association of India) for vehicle
tracking systems (VTS) and
emergency request buttons.

M2M+ Tracking Device is a compact, reliable solution for asset tracking and IoT. It offers global LTE Cat 4, multi-GNSS (GPS/GLONASS/
QZSS), and I/O interfaces (RS485, RS232, 1-Wire) for sensors, RFID, and cameras. IP50-rated, with OTA updates, a 600 mAh battery, and -10°C to +55°C operation.

AmEco is a compact, advanced 4G LTE Cat 1 tracker for vehicles and IoT. It offers multi-GNSS (GPS/BeiDou/Galileo/GLONASS), digital/
analog I/O, RS232, 1-Wire for temperature and RFID, and Bluetooth. IP50-rated, with FOTA updates, a 650 mAh battery, and -40°C to +85°C operation.
